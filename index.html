Readers of today, Leaders of tomorrow!

Reading made real, vibrant and current with a very wide range of print and digital text types from cultures around the world

Key Features

Reading

  • Text Types – wide-ranging types of texts
  • Themes – age-relevant themes
  • Learning ‘how to read’ and ‘what to read’
  • Reading comprehension
  • Vocabulary development

Critical Literacy involving
Multi-literacies

  • Digital literacy – navigate and read digital texts that are non-linear and hyper-linked
  • Trans-disciplinary literacy – read critically across subject areas
  • Social-Emotional Literacy (SEL) – identify, understand and express emotions; develop empathy

Global Citizenship

  • Train to be a discerning reader
  • Develop a sensitive, tolerant and inclusive world view
  • Multiculturalism - respect, appreciate and relate to different cultures

Benefits

To Students

  • Practise with self access, supported by answer key
  • Ace the CBSE's Reading Challenge, PISA and many competitive exams
  • Improve performance across school subjects
  • Develop creative and critical thinking (CCT) for life
  • Be an active and empathetic citizen of your country and the world

For the Teacher

  • Facilitate or monitor with minimal or no teaching load
  • Apply the same methodology and question formats while teaching other course books
  • Choose Basic or Advanced books or both, flexibly
  • Fit the eight levels flexibly to match the competency of the class
